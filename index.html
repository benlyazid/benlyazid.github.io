What is Think AI Morocco?

Think AI Morocco is a 4-Days AI hackathon co-hosted by 1337AI and Math&Maroc which brings together talented teams of students from different Moroccan schools to compete in challenges that showcase their creativity and expertise. Over the course of the hackathon, participants will have the opportunity to develop new skills, collaborate with like-minded individuals, and create innovative solutions that could make a real difference in the world. Don't miss out on this exciting opportunity to be part of the future of AI in Morocco!

Get ready for an unforgettable AI hackathon that combines the cutting-edge technology of AI with the rich and colorful culture of Morocco! Explore the endless possibilities of AI and showcase your skills in creating innovative solutions that can transform the world. Whether you're a seasoned AI expert or a budding enthusiast, this is the perfect opportunity to push the boundaries of your creativity and make a mark in the world of AI. Join us and be part of the next generation of AI pioneers

FAQ

  • Is it free?

    Yes, applying and competing in the hackathon is free.

  • Food ?

    Breakfast/Lunch/Dinner

  • Lodging?

    Free at UM6P

  • Skills needed?

    Bac +4 in AI related degrees and having worked on AI projects before.

  • When will I be contacted?

    On May 8th, you'll be contacted by email.

  • Will I need my laptop?

    You will be given access to workstations, but we recommend that you also bring your PC.

  • Can I participate alone?

    No, teams must be composed of 2-3 members
